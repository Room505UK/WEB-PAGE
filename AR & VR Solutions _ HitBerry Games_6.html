    What are AR & VR application? 

    A VR application is a headset-based app, that you could run standalone on devices like Oculus Quest 1/2, or PC-based apps that could be installed on your PC and realtime casted to your headset like HTC Vive. Both technologies have their pros and cons but the main idea that portable headsets can give user more freedom in his actions, but PC apps always have higher quality graphics and require powerful PC. An AR apps generally could be used on every modern smartphone with built-in camera. They allow user to spawn some 3D objects over real camera view.

    ​

    Why do you need XR app? 

    XR apps are very popular in the E-Learning industry, because they allow trainees to totally dive into the education process and engages their ability to use visual memory. The other reason to use an XR app is your money - it's much more cheaper to create easy to use and upgrade training software, than build a physical simulator. In addition, modern technologies like hand and eye tracking in VR headsets provide us with the ability to use new sources of input to control apps.
